02 · Brand

Logo, marks & app icons

The mark is two independent paths that meet, exchange, and continue — from lidh, “to connect”. Never a chain-link, torrent glyph, magnet, or infinity sign. Masters live in Brand/svg; raster from 16→1024 px in Brand/png.

Gradient — primary
White — on color / photo

✓ Do
  • Clear space = ¼ of the mark's width on all sides.
  • Min size: full mark 20px · mono mark 16px · lockup 180px.
  • Gradient on neutral surfaces; white on color/photography.
  • Teal starts the connection · green = movement · lime = terminal highlight only.
✕ Don't
  • Rotate, outline, stretch, or add external glows.
  • Recolor individual path segments.
  • Place the gradient mark on a competing green gradient.
  • Use piracy-coded language or magnet / pirate imagery.

Full rules: BRAND-GUIDELINES.md · platform masters in Brand/platform-icons.

05 · Concept & compliance

The thinking behind the pixels

The full narrative — user research, personas, the Tauri + Rust architecture, and the per-store compliance strategy — lives in the concept page.

iOS — PWA, don't submit

Apple bans torrent clients & remotes. Ship an installable Liquid-Glass PWA served from the user's own box.

macOS — notarize

Developer-ID signed & notarized, direct distribution — skip the sandboxed Mac App Store.

Android — full client

Play permits it. Content-neutral “downloads & data” framing throughout.
