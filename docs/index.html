Built for how you actually use it

The 90% is a glance. The 10% is full control.

Everything the power user needs, none of the 2009 clutter. Progressive by default - simple until you ask for more.

Menu-bar & tray glance

Check speed, pause, or add a transfer without ever opening a window. Your real home screen.

Debrid, built in

Bring your Real-Debrid, AllDebrid, or TorBox account - resolve magnets to fast, encrypted HTTPS. Or torrent locally.

Phone & watch

Native apps on Android and iOS, plus a watch glance. The same queue, on every device you own.

RSS, categories & rules

Auto-grab feeds, sort with categories and tags, and let rules file everything where it belongs.

Bandwidth control

Schedules, global caps, and per-transfer limits so Lidhra never fights your Zoom call.

Private by default

No accounts, no telemetry, no cloud. Signed, notarized, reproducible builds you can verify.

Cloud transfers · debrid

Torrenting without a torrent on your device.

Connect the debrid account you already have. Lidhra hands the magnet to the cloud - the peer-to-peer part happens on the provider's servers - and you get back a plain, TLS-encrypted HTTPS download. Fast, private, and nothing heavy touching your device or your network.

Tiny on purpose

Built on Tauri + Rust. It respects your RAM.

No bundled Chromium, no 400 MB idle footprint. Lidhra renders in your OS's own webview and does the heavy lifting in a compiled Rust core - so the installer is single-digit megabytes and it stays out of your way in the background.
