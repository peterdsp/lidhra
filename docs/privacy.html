Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 July 2026

Lidhra is a native download and transfer app that runs on your own device and your own debrid account. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information. It is short because there is very little to say.

The short version

Lidhra collects no personal data. There are no accounts to create with us, no analytics, no telemetry, no tracking, and no advertising. We do not operate servers that receive your activity, and we cannot see what you download.

Your debrid account

You connect a debrid account you already have (for example Real-Debrid, AllDebrid, TorBox or Premiumize). The credentials or API token you enter are stored locally on your device and are sent only to the debrid provider you chose, over an encrypted HTTPS connection, so the app can resolve your links. Those credentials are never sent to us and never leave your device for any other purpose.

Downloads

When you add a link, the resolving happens on your provider's servers and the resulting file is downloaded directly to your device over HTTPS. The links you add and the files you download stay on your device. We do not receive, log or store any of them.

Licensing

On the App Store, your purchase is verified by Apple's App Store receipt on your device; no information is sent to us. On the direct and Ko-fi builds, the email you enter to activate is sent once to our licence service only to confirm your purchase and issue your key; it is not used for anything else and is not shared.

Third parties

Lidhra bundles no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs. When you use the app it communicates only with the debrid provider you configured and the file hosts your downloads come from. Their handling of data is governed by their own privacy policies.

Children

Lidhra is a general-purpose utility and is not directed at children. It collects no personal data from anyone.

Data deletion

All app data, including your debrid credentials and settings, lives on your device. Removing the app deletes it. You can also disconnect your account from within the app at any time.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated date above will change. Material changes will be noted on this page.
